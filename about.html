About Open Scholar Bangladesh

Open Scholar Bangladesh is a student-run research platform that connects Bangladeshi scholars across borders. Our community blends peer support, mentor guidance, and open resources to make research education accessible.

Founder

Asif Nawaz

A management studies graduate who has mentored 200+ students with research and thesis projects. Asif built OSBD to bridge scholars with faculty and peer networks across nations.
