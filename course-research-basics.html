Course 1: Research Basics

A 10-session foundational pathway for emerging social science and business researchers.

  1. Understanding research questions and scope.
  2. Research ethics and responsible conduct.
  3. Finding and organizing academic sources.
  4. Building a literature review map.
  5. Qualitative vs. quantitative design choices.
  6. Sampling and data collection planning.
  7. Interview and survey fundamentals.
  8. Writing methodology sections.
  9. Data analysis introduction and tools.
  10. Formatting, citation styles, and submission tips.
