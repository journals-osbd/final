osbd.info · Student-led research network

Open Scholar Bangladesh connects learners, mentors, and research communities.

We are a student-run platform uniting Bangladeshi scholars at home and abroad. Our peer-led learning circles, YouTube classes, and mentorship network help social science and business research students build rigorous, ethical, and confident scholarship.
